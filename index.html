Math Junior


Game Instructions

  1. Press play to start the game.
  2. You can play the game with the deafult category chose i.e: Addition or you have the option to choose from the other categories(Subtraction, Multiply and Divide).
  3. Once you start the game, you will be given an airthmetic equation to solve with four different options to choose the correct answer from.
  4. Select the right answer from the displayed options.
  5. On choosing the right answer, you will be given a score of 1 as your correct answer.
  6. On choosing the wrong answer, you will be given a score of 1 as your incorrect answer.
  7. The aim of the game is to test your math knowledge and get as many correct as you want.
  8. There is no time limit, you can end or reset the game whenever you want.
  9. Enjoy and Best of Luck! :)
